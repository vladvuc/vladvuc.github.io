Scenarios and their HI-ness score

Exploring Human Expertise

In the following section, you will find two tables representing tasks involved in project management and software engineering, each accompanied by an assigned "HI-ness" score [2]. This score serves as an indicator of the degree of human involvement necessary for the successful execution of each task, illuminating the areas where human expertise plays a pivotal role and highlighting the potential effectiveness of automation or AI assistance.

The HI-ness score is defined as:

Weak: No or little collaboration

Strong: Collaboration and synergy exists

The first table focuses on project management tasks, encompassing activities like planning, resource allocation, and stakeholder communication. The second table delves into software engineering tasks, ranging from requirements gathering and design to coding and testing.
